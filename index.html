Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our tutoring marketplace.

How does the commission model work?
Tutors post ads completely free. When a student books and pays for a lesson through our platform, we deduct a 15% commission. The remaining 85% is paid to the tutor. There are no monthly fees, listing fees, or hidden charges.
Is posting a tutor ad really free?
Yes! Creating a tutor profile and posting ads is 100% free. You only pay commission when you successfully complete a paid lesson through our platform.
How do I get paid as a tutor?
Payments are processed securely through our platform. After a lesson is confirmed, funds (minus the 15% commission) are transferred to your connected bank account or PayPal within 3-5 business days.
Are tutors verified?
Yes. All tutors go through a verification process including ID checks, qualification verification, and background screening. Verified tutors display a green badge on their profile.
Can I take a trial lesson?
Many tutors offer a free introductory session or a discounted first lesson. Additionally, new tutors get their first lesson commission-free as a welcome bonus from us.
What subjects are covered?
We cover virtually every subject: Mathematics, Languages, Sciences, Music, Test Preparation, Programming, Art, and more. If you don't find your subject, you can request it and we'll help match you.
